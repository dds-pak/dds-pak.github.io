About StoryWorld

Display Gardens

Spread across 14 hectares encompassing 10 internationally themed gardens to explore, there is something for everyone, including the ever-popular Storybook Garden. 8km of pathways will lead you to spectacular vistas including a 10m high waterfall, topiary displays, man-made ponds, statues, murals, beautiful architectural structures and much more. In the Gardens there are 6,000 trees, 600,000 shrubs and over 1 million ground covers to discover from Australia and around the world.

Learn More

Amazing Rides

Open during school holidays and event periods our 5 permanent rides add an air of excitement and fun to the Gardens for the young and young at heart. The rides include the Swing Chair, Giant Ferris Wheel, Carousel, Superslide and Tea Cup. Rides are only available during event periods. Rides passes can be purchased at from the rides booth.

Learn More

Multiple Story Rooms

Our StoryWorld offers a unique and immersive experience for children, where they can explore and interact with their favorite stories in specially designed rooms. Each story room is carefully crafted to recreate the environment of a popular story, allowing children to step inside the tale and become part of the adventure. From Alice in Wonderland to Peter Pan, our story rooms feature a range of beloved stories that children can discover and enjoy. Each room is equipped with interactive activities that encourage children to use their imaginations and engage with the story in new and exciting ways.
