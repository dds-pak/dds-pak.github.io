Our StoryRooms

Humpty Dumpty Room

The Humpty Dumpty Story Room is a cozy and whimsical space that transports visitors to the world of the famous nursery rhyme character. Adorned with wall murals of Humpty Dumpty and his friends, the room is designed to stimulate the imagination of both children and adults alike.

Monstro the Whale

Have you ever wondered how Pinocchio felt when he was swallowed by the Monstro the whale? Find out for yourself by climbing inside Monstro’s giant mouth and see what treasures he has swallowed!

Wishing Well

Wishing wells are believed to grant wishes with the spoken word. When you make a wish at Storybook Gardens, make sure to say it out loud and maybe your wish will come true.

Located in the middle of Springbank Park, Storybook Gardens is owned and operated by the Council of London
