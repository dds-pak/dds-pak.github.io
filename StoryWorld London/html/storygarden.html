Welcome to StoryWorld Gardens

Step into a world of imagination and wonder at StoryWorld Gardens. Our gardens are filled with characters and scenes from your favorite stories, providing a magical experience for visitors of all ages.

Our Gardens

Pete’s Friend-Ship

Sail the high seas on Pete’s Friend-Ship – take the wheel, slide down the slides, or head below to see what’s kept in the hold.

Village Square Stage

You never know who will appear next on the Village Square Stage! Two outdoor stages provide the perfect setting for a variety of entertainment features throughout the summer including live musical performances, clowns, puppet shows, sing-a-longs, storytelling, and so much more! The Village Square Stage is a lively spot and fun for musicians, magicians, mascots and artists.

Gnorbets Garden

Come see what’s growing in Gnorbert’s Gardens! Garden programs and activities are scheduled daily, and you may even catch a glimpse of Gnorbert the Gnome!

Located in the middle of Springbank Park, Storybook Gardens is owned and operated by the Council of London
